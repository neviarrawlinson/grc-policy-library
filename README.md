# GRC Policy Library

A practical library of GRC policy templates for practitioners,
career changers, and governance teams building or improving their
policy programs.

Every template in this library is:
- Written in plain language — no unnecessary legal jargon
- Structured for real operational use — not just compliance checkboxes
- Aligned to major frameworks including ISO 27001, NIST CSF, and SOC 2
- Free to use, adapt, and share

---

## Who This Is For

- GRC analysts and managers building or updating a policy program
- Career changers building a GRC portfolio with real artifacts
- IT and security teams that need governance documentation fast
- Organizations preparing for ISO 27001, SOC 2, or internal audits

## How to Use These Templates

1. Download or copy the template you need
2. Replace all bracketed placeholders with your organization's
   specific information
3. Have the policy reviewed by your legal or compliance team
   before finalizing
4. Get formal approval from the appropriate leadership level
5. Distribute to all applicable personnel and obtain acknowledgment
6. Add to your policy review calendar — annually at minimum

These templates are starting points, not finished products. Every
organization's risk environment is different. Adapt them to fit
your context.

---

## Framework Alignment

These policies are designed with the following frameworks in mind:

- **ISO 27001:2022** — International standard for information
  security management systems
- **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)** — US government framework
  for managing cybersecurity risk
- **SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria** — AICPA standard for service
  organization controls
- **NIST SP 800-53** — Security and privacy controls for federal
  information systems
